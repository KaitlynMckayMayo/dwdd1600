Caramel Swirl Cheesecake Bars

Caramel Swirl Cheesecake Bars are a luscious twist on classic cheesecake, designed to be easy to make and shareable. These bars start with a buttery graham cracker crust that serves as a sturdy and slightly crunchy base for the creamy cheesecake filling. The star of the dessert is a generous swirl of rich caramel sauce layered throughout the cheesecake, which adds both visual appeal and a sweet, buttery depth of flavor.

  • heavy cream

Raspberry Swirl Shortbread Cookies

A Raspberry Swirl Shortbread Cookie is a delightful fusion of buttery, melt-in-your-mouth shortbread and the bright, fruity taste of raspberries. The cookie base is a classic shortbread, known for its rich, buttery flavor and tender, crumbly texture. Raspberry jam or fresh raspberry puree is swirled through the dough before baking, creating a beautiful marbled effect with vivid red swirls against the pale cookie backdrop.

Main Ingredients Needed:

  • rasberries
  • sugar
  • white chocolate chips
  • butter
  • vanilla
  • flour
  • salt
  • milk
  • cornstarch
  • lemon juice
